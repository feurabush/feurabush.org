Understanding American Democracy

Truth matters. Facts are not partisan. They exist independently of belief or ideology.

"Patriotism means to stand by the country. It does not mean to stand by the president." - Theodore Roosevelt

Democracy thrives when:

  • Citizens respect verified facts
  • Leaders accept electoral outcomes
  • Peaceful transfer of power occurs
  • No individual is above the law

Did you know?

The Constitution begins with "We the People" - not "Me the President" or "My Political Movement."

"A house divided against itself cannot stand." - Abraham Lincoln

Signs of authentic patriotism:

  • Respecting democratic institutions
  • Supporting free and fair elections
  • Defending the Constitution
  • Rejecting personality cults
  • Valuing truth over conspiracy

Stand for democracy. Stand for truth. Stand for America.
